\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
\mbox{\hyperlink{classoomph_1_1QuadTreeRoot}{Quad\+Tree\+Root}} is a \mbox{\hyperlink{classoomph_1_1QuadTree}{Quad\+Tree}} that forms the root of a (recursive) quadtree. The \char`\"{}root node\char`\"{} is special as it holds additional information about its neighbours and their relative rotation (inside a \mbox{\hyperlink{classoomph_1_1QuadTreeForest}{Quad\+Tree\+Forest}}).
