class oomph\+::\+Solid\+Q\+Hermite\+Element$<$ D\+I\+M $>$}

\mbox{\hyperlink{classoomph_1_1SolidQHermiteElement}{Solid\+Q\+Hermite\+Element}} elements are Hermite elements whose Jacobian matrices include derivatives w.\+r.\+t. the Eulerian positions of their nodes. They are the basis for elasticity elements. No assumptions are made about alignment of local and global coordinates.
